About Taslima AkterI am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of Texas at San Antonio, specializing in human–computer interaction, usable privacy and security, and accessibility. My research focuses on designing privacy-preserving, ethics-driven technologies with and for blind and low-vision people, using empirical studies and user-centered design to inform practical solutions. I completed my PhD in Computer Science at Indiana University Bloomington and served as a Computing Innovation Fellow postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Irvine. Over the past several years I have published research, collaborated across disciplines, and mentored students at multiple levels, with the goal of advancing inclusive, trustworthy technologies and preparing the next generation of researchers and practitioners.
