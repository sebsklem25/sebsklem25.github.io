About Taslima Akter

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of Texas at San Antonio, specializing in human-computer interaction, usable privacy and security, and accessibility. My research focuses on designing privacy-preserving, ethics-driven technologies with and for blind and low-vision people, using empirical studies and user-centered design to inform practical solutions. I completed my PhD in Computer Science at Indiana University Bloomington and served as a Computing Innovation Fellow postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Irvine. I publish research, collaborate across disciplines, and mentor students with the goal of advancing inclusive and trustworthy technologies.
